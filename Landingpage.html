About Us


Tirachim Sushi House was established in May 2022, Where we opened the doors of our first restaurant in Brunei Darussalam. Over the month, we have fortune to grow along side the Bruneian community and expanded to 5 branches across the region.

We serve an extensive variety of Japanese food & beverages that includes fresh sashimi, sushi, donburi, noodles, bento boxes and so on. We prioritize affordability, quality and freshness with our ingredients, and only serve value-for-money products which are primarily air-flown from Japan and Norway.

Now, Tirachim Sushi House is not only serving dine-in & take-away transactions, we also accommodate for other services that include delivery and catering.
